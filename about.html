About Us

Welcome to Born Smart Poultry Farm based in Matisi-Kitale, Kenya. We are passionate poultry farmers specializing in the rearing of Kienyeji and Improved Kienyeji hens from day-old chicks to maturity. Our farm is committed to delivering high-quality eggs, meat, and breeding stock to poultry lovers across the region.

We focus on natural rearing methods, nutritious feeding, and biosecure practices to ensure the health of our flock. Customers can choose hens of different sizes and ages based on their needs. Our aim is to provide sustainable, healthy, and profitable poultry solutions for everyone.
